DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

The department of computer science was established in 1995. It is one of the premier department under Mahatma Gandhi University with 13 dynamic, well-experienced faculty. Asst. Proffesor Mr. Binu M.B is the head of the department.

The department aims for the academic excellence and personality development so as to equip the students for placement. The placement cell of the department arrange special training programme.

Salent Features

  1. XCareer Guidance and PLacement cell with excellent placement record
  2. Tie-Up with IPSR Solution Ltd., for training and placement
  3. Training in modern IT technologies like PHP, Dotnet, Android, etc..
  4. Every year 90% of students get placed in various companies
